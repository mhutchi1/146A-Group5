About Our Team

We are a team of first year computer science/cybersecurity majors at Stevens Institute of Technology helping people realize their dream of finding spooky places around NYC and Hoboken. Whether it be for some Halloween fun, historical research, or regular curiousity, our website works to bring haunted places right to your device with locations, reviews, and ratings to streamline your experience. Happy H(a)unting!
